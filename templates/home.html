Welcome to SentilyZer

Want to know what's trending more and what's not among the people around you? Well, how about people's choices in faraway geographic locations?

Get Started

We are a team of Technology Enthusiasts from IIT Kanpur

We have developed SentilyZer to help you know your peer's opinion on any topic that you want.

Tweets

It examines the enormous amount of data(relevant to your topic and the restrictions you impose) accessible via Twitter.

Sentiment Analysis

After reasonable preprocessing of the data, it subjects the data to Sentiment Analysis, with the help of machine learning.

Subjectivity and Polarity

All in all, we draw up data that show how people's commentary changed in their subjectivity and polarity. Try out to find a lot more.

Services

These are the three services currently offered by SentilyZer to its users.

Search

To use this feature, enter the topic you wish to search and an interval (defined by a start date and an end date). Then hit Enter.

Compare

For this, provide the two topics you wish to compare and an interval (defined by a start date and an end date). For instance, comparing opinions of people on Covaxin versus Covishield between the period 1/5/21 till 1/6/21

Search by GeoLocation

Enter the search topic, a defined interval, and click on a location on the google-map to get the results for that geolocation. Hit Enter to know the analysis.
